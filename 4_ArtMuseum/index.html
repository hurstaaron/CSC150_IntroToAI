About the Museum

The Vision

◆

The Obsidian Gallery is a curated online art museum exploring the creative frontier of generative AI. Each piece in this collection was crafted using text-to-image AI models, guided by human imagination and artistic vision.

The themes draw from grimdark science fiction, high fantasy, abstract expressionism, and epic narrative art — worlds where heroes stand against impossible odds and color itself becomes a language of emotion.

This museum was created as part of an academic exploration into AI-generated art. All images were generated using DALL·E by OpenAI.
